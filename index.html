About Me

Meredith Grimes is a go-getter who has a yearning for knowledge. She attended NC State University where she earned a B.S. in Fashion Development and Product Management from the College of Textiles and a minor in Art and Design from the College of Design. She further developed skills in Graphic Design, Product Development, Sourcing, and while involved with three various internships in college. After graduation, she was a Visual Merchandiser, Operations and Customer Service manager, and Freelance Designer for three years. She has expertise in training, operations, metrics, sales, and engaging with customers creating a positive experience.

Her passion for learning and eagerness to build on her academic foundations has led her to earning a certificate for Coding and Web Development at UNC Charlotte through their Continuing Education program. She hopes to build on this skillset to positively impact the technology field.

Meredith believes that teamwork is one of the key building blocks for success and accepts any challenge she faces with a positive mindset. She is currently working as a Freelance Designer and Part Time Student with hopes to pursue a career as a Web Development professional. Meredith would love to collaborate on ideas or discuss a job opportunity. Contact her via email or LinkedIn to connect.
