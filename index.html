About Me

Meredith Grimes attended NC State University and earned a B.S. in Fashion Development and Product Management from the College of Textiles and a minor in Art and Design from the College of Design. She further developed skills in Graphic Design, Product Development and Sourcing while involved with three various internships in college. After graduation, she was a Visual Merchandiser, Personal Stylist and Freelance Designer for three years.

Her passion for learning and eagerness to build on her academic foundations has led her to currently studying Coding and Web Development at UNC Charlotte through their Continuing Education program.

Meredith believes that teamwork in the workplace is one of the key building blocks for success and accepts any challenge she faces with a positive mindset. She is currently working as a Freelance Designer and Part Time Student while with hopes to pursue a career as a web development professional when she graduates in April 2020.
